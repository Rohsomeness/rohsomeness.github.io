How to explore

  • Move — Arrow keys / WASD, or on-screen pad
  • Explore — Walk close to things; info opens automatically
  • Career — Board the ship, fly up past job planets
  • Home — Esc (from a room or the ship) returns to the hub
  • Coins — Stand on a spot for 5s (watch the ring). Collect all for sunglasses!
